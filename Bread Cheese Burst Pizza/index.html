Bread Cheese Burst Pizza

Bread based pizza recipes are extremely simple to make and can cater for all age groups and for all part of the day’s meal. These are generally made with pizza sauce applied on top of bread slices but can also be done as cheese burst with cheese stuffed between bread slices and topped with pizza toppings.

Preparation

1. Firstly, in a pan take 1 cup cream, ½ cup milk and 1 cup milk powder. Stir and mix well making sure there are no lumps.
2. Continue to cook on low flame without burning and keep stirring until the mixture thickens and separates from the pan.
3. Instant mawa is ready, you can use immediately or refrigerate and use up to a week.
4. Then in a large kadai heat 2-litre milk, ¼ tsp saffron. Stir and get the milk to a boil, without burning.
5. Continue to cook on low flame until the milk thickens.
6. Now simmer for 15 minutes or until the milk reduces to a quarter. After the milk thickens add prepared mawa and mix well.
7. Also, add ½ cup sugar and cook for 2 minutes on low flame. Now add ½ tsp cardamom powder, 2 tbsp cashew, 2 tbsp almonds and 2 tbsp pistachios.
8. Mix well and the kulfi mixture is ready to set. Pour the kulfi mixture into kulfi moulds. if you do not have moulds, you can pour until matka or glass cups.
9. Cover and freeze for 8 hours or until sets completely. After 8 hours, the kulfi has set completely and is ready to serve.
10. Finally, garnish the malai kulfi with few chopped pistachios and enjoy.
